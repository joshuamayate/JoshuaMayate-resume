Hey, I am Josh but I also go by Nobu!

I'm a Research Assistant by day, then a Video Game Streamer by night.

I advocate for mental health and share my passion about technology and video games on Twitch.tv!

I dream of being involved with the growth of technology and strive to utilize my understanding of empathy to intersect people with their electronics.

When I'm done with all my lab work I stream video games to a live audience on Twitch.tv/nobusen!
